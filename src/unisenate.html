University Senate

Powers, Duties & Responsibilities of the University Of Hargeisa Senate:

  1. Examine and approve the academic calendar of the University
  2. Approve the Universityâ€™s various academic programs
  3. Decide on the conferring of degrees, diplomas and certificates as well as medal
  4. Approve criteria for admission and enrollment of students
  5. Set criteria for the determination of academic standards
  6. Oversee quality assurance and excellence of programs
  7. Provide governing guidelines for the settlement of disciplinary problems
  8. Determine criteria for graduation and its ceremony processions

The Members Of The University Of Hargeisa Senate:

  1. The President who shall be the Chairperson;
  2. The Vice-president for Academic and Research ;
  3. The Vice President for Administrative and Development
  4. University Registrar
  5. Deans of Colleges, Schools, institutes Directors, Center Directors of the university
  6. Student Service and Counseling Director
  7. Academic Program Development and Promotion Director
  8. Examination Center Director
